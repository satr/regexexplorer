The description of operating modes - " To check up under the list " (Batch check)

" To check up under the list " - check on conformity of the list of lines

  • Input of a line of the target text
  • Editing of a line of the target text
  • Removal of a line or several lines of the target text
  • Check of lines of the target text on conformity to a pattern

Check of the list of lines on compatibility with a pattern of regular expression

The Operating procedure:

  1. In section "Target texts list" in a field of input to enter the text which will be checked on conformity to a pattern
  2. To press button "Add" - the text line will be added in the list below, in a column "Target text".
  3. If necessary to enter such similarly necessary quantity of checked lines
  4. Into a field "Regex pattern" to enter the text of a pattern
  5. To press button "Check" or a key "Enter" (if the cursor is in a field of input of a pattern)
  6. In a field "Result" the result of check will be displayed: "N matches (from M lines)" or "No matches"
  7. In the list "Target text " in a column "Result" will be displayed result of check of a collection of checked lines of the text, On conformity to a pattern of regular expression: "MUTCH" or "False"
Input of a line of the target text

In section "Target texts list" in a field of input to enter the text which will be checked on conformity to a pattern

To press button "Add"

The text the line will be added in the list below, in a column "Target text"

After addition of the text in the list, a field of input it will be cleared

For addition of one more line into a field of input to enter the new text

Inclusion of a flag "Keep the text after addition" will allow to keep the added text in a field of input

To press button "Add"

The line will be added in the list

The checkbox "Keep the text after addition" allows to enter a series of the lines having little changes (For example variants of the electronic addresses, differing amount of symbols: "a@a.a", "a@a.aa", "a@aa.aa").

Editing of a line of the target text

To locate the cursor of the mouse at a line of the text which needs to be edited

To click the left key of the mouse - a line of the list will become an edited text field

To change the text


To press key Enter - the changed text to be kept on a place of the former list in line


Pressing key ESC cancels change of a line

Removal of a line or several lines of the target text

For removal it is necessary to allocate the necessary line (or some lines, keeping key Shift or Ctrl)

Removal is made by pressing of a key "Delete" or item of the menu "Clear"

If any line is not allocated - it is offered to remove all the line long
