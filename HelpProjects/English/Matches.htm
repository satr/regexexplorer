The description of operating modes - "Matches" (the tab "Matches/Captions")

"Matches" - sample of a collection of lines matched to a pattern

Allocation from the text (including multilower case) collections of the lines which mutch to a pattern of regular expression

The Operating procedure:

  1. Into a field "Target text" to enter the text which will be checked on conformity to a pattern
  2. Into a field "Regex pattern" to enter the text of a pattern
  3. To press button "Check" or a key "Enter" (if the cursor is in a field of input of a pattern)
  4. In a field "Result" will be displayed result of check: "N matches " or "No matches"
  5. In the list of "Matches" the collection lines from the checked text, which mutch to a pattern of regular expression
    6. will be displayed
