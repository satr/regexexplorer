The description of operating modes - "IsMatch"

Check of the text (including multilower case) on compatibility with a pattern of regular expression

Into a field "Target text" to enter the text which will be checked on conformity to a pattern

Into a field "Regex pattern" to enter the text of a pattern

To press button "Check" or a key "Enter" (if the cursor is in a field of input of a pattern)

If the text mismatches a pattern of regular expression, in a field "Result" the result of check will be displayed: "Fault")

To change the text of a pattern

To press button "Check" or a key "Enter" (if the cursor is in a field of input of a pattern)

If the text is match to a pattern of regular expression, in a field "Result" the result of check will be displayed: "MATCH"
