Introduction

The product RegexExplorer is intended for development of patterns of regular expressions.

Options

  • Check of an any line of the text (including multilower case) on conformity to a pattern. It is idle time way to check up correctness of a pattern or conformity to it of the concrete text
  • Sample of an any line of the text (including multilower case) substrings, corresponding a pattern. A mode is most useful to information search in a log-files. Loading of any files of data is possible
  • Check of a set of lines of the text, on conformity to a pattern - each line is checked irrespective of others. This mode is useful at development of patterns of check of standard fields of the input having of many variants admissible contents - names, post and electronic addresses, phone numbers, etc.

Other options

  • Reception of a collection of the symbols used in the list of checked lines. It is useful for development of a pattern on already available data.
  • Input of special symbols (including UNICODE codes).
  • Collection of own patterns.
  • Preservation of history of development of patterns.
  • Preservation history of loaded files.
  • Loading of data from a clipboard or from a file with the list.
  • Preservation of the list of checked lines in a file.
